About The Last Cake on Earth

The Last Cake on Earth is a 2D top-down ARPG set post-apocalypse in the ruins of Cannoli City achieving your goal of baking the last cake on earth.

The Last Cake on Earth
Welcome to Cannoli City

Society has long since crumbled, and Cannoli City has fallen into ruin. You take control of the baker, Cannoli City's one remaining resident. Rather, its only human resident. Mutant sweets flood the streets, looking for prey. In spite of the danger, the baker must take to the streets to undertake their one true mission: to bake THE LAST CAKE ON EARTH to celebrate their birthday.


Explore

A wide open city awaits, filled with loot and monsters secret cafes hold valuable materials and powerful new weapons. Find clues that unravel the mystery of Cannoli City's demise A vast adventure awaits!

Conquer

Wield the tools of the baker to defeat mutant sweets treats and coffee scavenged from the city's remains provide powerful abilities. Venture into the Four Factories and vanquish powerful bosses to collect the ingredients needed to bake your cake.

Create

Restore your bakery to its former glory! Build workstations to store more items, improve your weapons, craft new sweets, and bake your final cake. Find coffee beans found in the city to brew new drinks to aid your quest!

Cooperate

The Last Cake on Earth will support player hosted multiplayer sessions for up to 4 people. Explore the City Streets with friends!
