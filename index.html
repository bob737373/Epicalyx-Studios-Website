Welcome to The Last Cake on Earth

The Last Cake on Earth is a 2D RPG set in a post-acolyptic society about achieving your goal of baking the last cake on earth.

About The Last Cake on Earth

Society has crumbled, so you know what you must do. Explore the city and build up your bakery in an effort to bake the last cake on earth.

Explore

Explore every corner of the city to find secrets and powerful resources.

Conquer

Venture into the dungeons and vanquish your foes to collect the ingredients needed to bake your cake.

Survive

Death in the dungeons can be a great setback.

Cooperate

The Last Cake on Earth supports player hosted multiplayer sessions for up to 4 people.
