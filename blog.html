Starting Development

2/18/2022

Hello! This is our first blog post for the development of our game, The Last Cake on Earth. We are currently discussing the extent of features in the game and dividing responsibilities. Our glorious team leader, the Sax God Robert Fahey, has been working hard to guide us in our roles and responsibilities.

We will be making this game fully 2D in Unity and making most of the assets ourselves, so we will be populating the website and blog with pictures and info as we go through development.

Check back soon for more updates! The game should be completed to a post-alpha state by the end of the semester.
